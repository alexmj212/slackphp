DKP Bot

Give or take DKP (Dragon Kill Points)

Is someone not calculating their DPS correctly? Are you a nazi raid leader with delusions of grandeur?

All users start with 500 DKP and points can be added or subtracted in increments no greater than 10 from all users.

  • /dkp [@username] [number]
    • Can be any person on your team, must start with "@"
    • Number must be a value between -10 and 10
    • Watch out, don't try to give yourself DKP
  • /dkp score
    • Displays your current DKP value
  • /dkp rank
    • Displays DKP Leaderboard for your team

Tip Bot

Drop a tip in someones jar

Did someone post a cool link or give some helpful advice? Give them a tip to thank them.

All users start with 0 tips. Tips cannot be taken, only given.

  • /tip [@username]
    • Can be any person on your team, must start with "@"
  • /tip total
    • Displays the total in your tip jar
